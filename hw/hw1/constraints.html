Home Affordances Constraints Signifiers
Physical Actions that are constrained by physical limitations, such as only being able to move a vertical scroll bar vertically.
Semantic Actions that are constrained by contextual sematic meaning, such as not shouting during an exam.
Logical Actions that are constrained by logical reasoning, such as looking for a hidden object in a room with four visible objects after being told there are five total.
Cultural Actions that are constrained by established cultural conventions, such as hyperlinks being underlined on websites.
Project Actions that are constrained by project limitation, such as budget, timeline, company policy, etc.
