I will introduce three topics about Human Perception & Cognition within the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI):

  1.  Affordances

    This page will define both real and perceived affordances with examples.

  2.  Constraints

    This page will explain the difference between physical, sematic, cultural, and project constraints.

  3.  Signifiers

    This page will define signifiers, cultural signifiers, and their use in design.

The information on these topics is provided by Don Norman, who is known as the "grandfather of human factors".
